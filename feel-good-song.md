# Light Through the Leaves
### A Feel-Good Folk Song

**Chords:** C – Em – Am – G/C – F

*(Pick through C – Em – Am – F, twice)*

## Verse 1
*(C)* Woke up slow this morning, coffee going cold,
*(Em)* Sun was pouring golden through the curtains I just rolled,
*(Am)* Nothing on my schedule, nowhere I need to be,
*(F)* Just the day wide open, just the wind and me.

---

## Verse 2
*(C)* Took a walk down to the river, watched the water run,
*(Em)* Every ripple catching sparks of afternoon sun,
*(Am)* An old man on the bench there gave a wave and smiled,
*(F)* Funny how a stranger makes the world feel like a child.

---

## Chorus
*(C)* Oh, the light through the leaves,
*(G/C)* Dancing easy in the breeze,
*(Am)* Like the world is singing soft and low,
*(F)* Telling me to let it go.

*(C)* And I feel it in my chest,
*(G/C)* This quiet kind of blessed —
*(Am)* Like every little thing is right where it belongs,
*(F)* And I'm right where I belong.
